Philosophy of Mental Disorder Discussion Group

This discussion group explores the philosophy of mental disorder from the perspective of patient experiences and with the aim of learning practical lessons for how we can better understand what it is like to have a mental disorder and how to treat it well.

This year our main focus will be the Mental Capacity Act (2005) and its implications. Last year, we met in person at King’s College London to read papers on phenomenology. Following Covid-19 and university strikes we moved our last meeting to Zoom. We will start back up again in September 2020, either in person or on Zoom, depending on the circumstances. Our tradition of free Reese’s peanut butter cups will be difficult to continue over Zoom but we will find a way if need be.

Find us on Facebook for event and reading list updates. We are open to topic suggestions and welcome attendees to present on topics they are interested in.
